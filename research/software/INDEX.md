# Consolidated Research Index

This directory serves as the centralized hub for all research related to the Irish EdTech platform, Celtic Language AI, and infrastructure development.

## Core Research Modules

### 1. [Document Intelligence & VLM Fine-Tuning](./document-intelligence-vlm.md)
- **Topics:** Qwen3-VL, Docling, STEM extraction, philological fine-tuning for Celtic scripts.
- **Goal:** High-fidelity digitization of Irish/English exam papers and curricula.

### 2. [Infrastructure & Knowledge Graph Platform](./infrastructure-knowledge-graph.md)
- **Topics:** DuckLake (DuckDB/Iceberg), Cognee (OWL), Graphiti (Temporal), Komodo/Pangolin.
- **Goal:** A secure, unified backend for storing and querying educational knowledge.

### 3. [Celtic Language AI Resources](./celtic-language-ai-ml.md) (Existing)
- **Topics:** Statistics on Irish, Welsh, Scottish Gaelic, and Manx resources.
- **Goal:** Cataloging available models and datasets on HuggingFace.

### 4. [Irish EdTech Platform Architecture](./irish-edtech-platform.md) (Consolidated)
- **Topics:** BAML schemas, marking scheme logic, and bilingual platform strategy.
