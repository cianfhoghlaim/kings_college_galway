# Celtic Language Education Enrollment Statistics

## Overview

This document provides detailed enrollment data for Celtic medium education across all jurisdictions, with trend analysis and comparative metrics.

---

## 1. Wales - Welsh Medium Education

### 1.1 Overall Statistics (January 2025)

| Category | Schools | Pupils | % of Total |
|----------|---------|--------|------------|
| **Welsh Medium** | 405 | 93,377 | 21% |
| **Dual Language** | 66 | 23,807 | 5% |
| **English Medium** | 933 | 336,166 | 74% |
| **Total** | 1,404 | 453,350 | 100% |

**Source:** Schools' census results: January 2025, Welsh Government

### 1.2 Geographic Distribution

Welsh-medium as default provision:
- Gwynedd
- Isle of Anglesey
- Ceredigion
- Carmarthenshire

Minority provision (17 local authorities):
- English-medium dominant
- Welsh taught as subject

### 1.3 Trend Analysis

| Year | WM Pupils | % of Total | Change |
|------|-----------|------------|--------|
| 2020 | ~91,000 | 20.5% | - |
| 2022 | ~92,500 | 20.8% | +1.5K |
| 2025 | 93,377 | 21.0% | +0.9K |

**Assessment:** Stable but below trajectory needed for Cymraeg 2050 (40% target)
