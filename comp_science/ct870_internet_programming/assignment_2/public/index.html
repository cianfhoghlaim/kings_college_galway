Howler Letter and Floo Powder networks disrupted due to Death Eater attack

06/10/2021

Message forwarded from ISS: NUIG Aurors have detected suspicious activity in our magical communication networks. As a result, all Howler-enchanted letters and all Floo Powder access points have been temporarily shutdown to ensure the safety of students' data. It is not known at this time whether previous communications have been intercepted. Quidditch training will continue as planned, however, but students must register via post or e-mail. For those students not accustomed to muggle communication methods, please follow this link for further information.

Council called into Question

03/10/2021

An official complaint has been registered against NUIG Quidditch Club for electing all 2021 Quidditch Council members from Gryffindor House. We refute any accusations of bias, but next week we will be electing a fifth council member to the brand new role of Public Relations Officer. Join us for the public vote at 3 o'clock on Thursday 07/11/2021 in Smokey's!

Remember-All found in locker room

10/09/2021

A silver Remember-All has been found in the locker room and has been handed in to NUIG's lost & found. Please note that the Accio spell will not work on university grounds and it will have to be picked up in-person.

Sharing the Snitch

01/05/2021

A reminder that the Golden Snitch will be unavailable for training sessions throughout the summer, as it is on loan to the Enchanting club to help new 1st Years learn about the Flight charm.

Quidditch for all!

01/02/2021

NUIG Quidditch Club is a proud member of the International Quidditch Association, we believe that everyone should be allowed play Quidditch regardless of their magical ability. We have specialist coaches available to train muggle students who might not have used enchanted brooms before, ALL NUIG students are welcome! Please get in touch with our Secretary for further information.
