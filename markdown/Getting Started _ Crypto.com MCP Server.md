## Getting Started

Learn how to connect the Crypto.com Market Data MCP server to your AI assistant

## Welcome!

The **Crypto.com Market Data MCP Server** lets you access real-time cryptocurrency market information directly in your AI assistant conversations. Whether you’re using ChatGPT or Claude, you can ask questions about crypto prices, market trends, and trading data.

## What is MCP?

**MCP** stands for **Model Context Protocol**. Think of it as a way for your AI assistant (like ChatGPT or Claude) to connect to external services and get fresh, real-time information.

Without MCP, your AI assistant only knows information from its training data. With MCP, it can:

- Get current cryptocurrency prices
- Check market trends
- Access trading volumes
- And much more!

## What Can You Do With This?

Once you’ve set up the Crypto.com Market Data MCP server, you can ask your AI assistant questions like:

- “What’s the current price of Bitcoin?”
- “Show me the top 10 cryptocurrencies”
- “What’s the 24-hour trading volume for Ethereum?”
- “Compare the prices of Bitcoin and Ethereum”
- “What are the trending cryptocurrencies today?”

Your AI assistant will fetch real-time data from Crypto.com and give you accurate, up-to-date answers!

## How Does It Work?

Here’s a simple overview:

1. **You configure your AI app** - You add a small piece of configuration to ChatGPT or Claude
2. **The app connects to the MCP server** - Your AI assistant connects to `https://mcp.crypto.com/market-data/mcp`
3. **You ask questions** - You chat normally with your AI assistant
4. **Real-time data is fetched** - When you ask about crypto, the AI gets fresh data from Crypto.com
5. **You get accurate answers** - The AI responds with current market information

### Completely Free

This service is completely free to use. You don’t need to create an account, get an API key, or provide any payment information.

## What You’ll Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

### For ChatGPT Users:

- **ChatGPT account**
- **Web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- **Internet connection**

### For Claude Users:

- **Claude account**
- **Web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- **Internet connection**
